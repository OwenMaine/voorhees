Welcome

Welcome to Voorhees Manufacturing

Based in Newark, Maryland, Voorhees Rubber Manufacturing Co Inc is a leading manufacturer of
custom rubber and plastic products, delivering innovative solutions for diverse industrial applications.
Our commitment to quality and precision defines our manufacturing excellence.

Our Mission

To provide superior rubber and plastic manufacturing solutions through advanced technology and expertise. We are dedicated to delivering high-quality products that meet precise specifications and exceed customer expectations, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Our Vision

To be the industry leader in rubber and plastic manufacturing, setting benchmarks for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. We aim to continuously expand our capabilities, embrace cutting-edge technologies, and maintain our position as a trusted partner in manufacturing excellence.
