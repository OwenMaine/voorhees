FAQs

Common Questions

  • What types of rubber and plastic products do you manufacture?
    We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of rubber and plastic products, including custom molded parts, industrial components, seals, gaskets, and specialized plastic products. Our manufacturing capabilities allow us to work with various materials and specifications to meet diverse industry needs.
  • Can you develop custom products to meet specific requirements?
    Yes, we offer comprehensive custom product development services. Our team of engineers works closely with clients to understand their requirements, develop prototypes, and create solutions that meet exact specifications. We handle everything from design to final production.
  • What quality standards do you follow?
    We maintain strict quality control standards throughout our manufacturing process. Our facility adheres to industry standards and quality management systems. Each product undergoes rigorous testing and inspection before delivery to ensure it meets specifications.
  • What is your typical production lead time?
    Lead times vary depending on the product complexity and order volume. Standard products typically require 2-4 weeks, while custom products may take 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the quotation process and offer expedited services when possible.
  • Do you provide material recommendations?
    Yes, our technical team provides expert material recommendations based on your application requirements, including factors like temperature resistance, chemical exposure, durability needs, and cost considerations. We help select the optimal materials for your specific use case.
  • What is your minimum order quantity?
    Minimum order quantities vary by product type and manufacturing process. We accommodate both small and large production runs, and work with clients to find the most cost-effective solution for their needs. Contact us for specific product MOQs.
